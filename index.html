Why study in
Australia

Australia has one of the best education systems in the world that offers a high quality education with a lot of top ranked universities. Australia also offers great work opportunities as students have the ability to stay in Australia after their graduation, taking advantage of the post-study work visa. Good employment opportunities existing within Australia will also make it possible for students to gain permanent residence. Australian culture is highly diverse, which means you will get to study with students from all over the world, discover different cultures and enjoy many of the comforts of home. Melbourne and Sydney are among the top 10 cities for students according to The QS Best Student Cities 2019 and are also recognized as the 2nd and 3rd most liveable cities in the world according to The Global Liveability Index 2019.

Frequently Asked Questions

Every Question carries its own answer. We have Some Frequently Asked Questions.

Where and how can I get information about studying abroad?

Attend a counselling session with us where all the valuable information about studying overseas will be provided to you by our experts and find out different study options. You can choose what is best for you from these alternatives.

Can I apply to courses in different universities?

Yes. Once you have selected your universities and courses of interest, we will act on your behalf and lodge the applications of admission to the universities of your choice. Documents needed for your application process will be carefully chosen and presented by our team. You will be informed as soon as we receive the acceptance letters from the universities you applied to.

How much do you charge for the application process?

Unikey Global won't charge you for the application process. Yes, you heard it right, It's ABSOLUTELY FREE! In other words, it will cost the same as you would have applied directly but as a bonus, you will get the opportunity to receive scholarships and bursaries when applying through Unikey Global.

Which days are you available?

We are ready to help you throughout the weekdays from 9 AM to 5 PM and on weekends by appointment.

Can I work in Australia while I’m studying?

While you are on a student visa you are allowed to work 20 hours per week during the study period and unlimited hours on holidays. We will even help you find part-time opportunities if required.

What documents should I take with me to Australia?

You will need your passport, the offer letter/ copy of the COE from your university, proof of previous academic qualifications, and the orientation letter. Additionally, make sure you are well aware of the climate, cost of living, and customs/ quarantine to make necessary arrangements for the departure. We will arrange airport pickups and accomodations in Australia if required.
