Our Services

Home Services

Counselling for Course and Institution Selection

Our counsellors provide detailed information and guidance to students who intend to study in Australia.

As Unikey global represents many recognized universities and institutions, extensive details about the availability of different courses, their academic requirements, and the costs are provided to you. Making you aware of all the aspects of studying in Australia is important to both you and us as it will help in making the right decision for your study path.

We consider your educational qualifications and the financial background in order to find the best course and university for you. This avoids rejections from universities and other unnecessary issues in the admission process. We always make sure to inform you on various scholarships and fee waivers offered too.

Enrolment Process

Our experts will assist you in the university enrolment process in the best manner by simplifying the application process.

The application documents will be compiled by our team with utmost care on behalf of you which makes the admission process much less tedious and more efficient and accurate.

We guarantee that you will always be provided with updated and relevant information on courses and university requirements.

Visa Processing

Visa processing can be challenging and confusing, so we take over the responsibility of the lodging of visa applications and obtaining a student visa with no delays and cancellations for you.

We guarantee that all visa requirements will be addressed accurately and the documents will be properly analyzed and reviewed before the lodging of the visa application.

This will be a completely hassle-free process for you as we make sure to represent you in the most trustworthy and appropriate manner to the respective authorities.

Other Services

We will ensure that you will have all the necessary needs addressed by the time you arrive in Australia. This includes helping you settle down in Australia by finding accommodation, providing airport pickup, and helping find part-time jobs. Additionally, we also provide parent and spouse visas.
