Unikey Global

Australia is home to a large number of globally reputed universities that offer a wide range of higher education opportunities. For a prospective student, the decisions that need to be taken can be very daunting since it’s not just the course you’re interested in that matters when choosing a university. Your existing educational qualifications and the financial background also plays a role in these decisions.

This choice is a very important one for your future, and that’s why we’re here to help. The defining purpose of Unikey Global is to guide students like yourself through this journey and help you achieve your higher education goals.

Unikey Global is an educational consultancy established in 2007, and we are a team of passionate and well experienced consultants who want to help students of any calibre realize their true potential.

We provide services ranging from professional counselling to visa guidance to our clientele and the quality and integrity of our services have placed us among the best educational consultancies today. We represent many universities in Australia and have the ability to act as an intermediary between you and your interested universities in order to provide a smooth selection and transition process.

Message from the Founder

It is important to have proper guidance as international students besides the study path you choose to secure a future career. We at Unikey Global ensure a bright future for all students who join us to walk them through the journey of higher studies starting from the admission process to visa processing.

Given the high number of reputed universities and institutes in Australia, it can be very challenging for any student to find the proper course and educational provider as per their interest and previous academic qualifications. Therefore it is very important to have someone professional to guide you in the correct direction to achieve your goals. This is the main vision of Unikey Global. We are a team of highly qualified student counsellors who are experts in the field with good years of work experience. We guide students for the selection of the course and institute and takes the responsibility of admission process to visa processing. Also, we go beyond our ways to help students settle down in Australia by finding accommodation, providing airport pickup, and helping to find part-time jobs. We ensure a student will have all the necessary needs addressed by the time he or she arrives in Australia.
