Accounting and Finance

Accounting and Finance is a major part of any business. Knowledge in this field provides a range of career pathways to students who are aspiring to be at the top in the leading organizations of the world.

In the 2019 QS World University Rankings: Accounting and Finance, 6 Australian universities were cited in the Top 50 band and a total of 17 Australian universities were cited in the Top 200.

The Times Higher Education World University Rankings 2019 has cited 24 Australian universities in the Top 500 band.

Knowledge is Accounting and Finance is globally recognized and rewarded and Melbourne is home to many institutes and universities that offer related courses which are known for the quality and employability outcomes.

These courses creates a direct path to becoming a professional accountant and getting accreditation from professional bodies like CPA.

Available Courses


  • Diploma of Accounting
  • Associate Degree of Accounting
  • Graduate Diploma in Accounting
  • Advanced Diploma of Accounting
  • Bachelor of Accounting
  • Master of Professional Accounting

CPA Australia is one of the world’s most prominent accounting bodies and CPA associate membership can be obtained by Accounting and Finance graduates after satisfying all the requirements and foundation tests. More details about obtaining the associate membership can be found in CPA publications and the website.


Information Technology

It is no surprise that the Information Technology industry is among the most popular. Fast growing, and lucrative industries in today’s technology focused world. Since Information Technology is used in almost every industry, knowledge in this field provides a multitude of amazing opportunities for students to seize.

Melbourne is Australia's technology hub and home to more than half of the country’s top 20 tech companies, while the state of Victoria has over 8,000 IT businesses based there, including IBM, Microsoft and Intel.

The demand for IT professionals is only rising and fields like Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Data Science, and Software Development are popular around the world.

As there are many globally recognized universities and institutes in Australia that provides accredited Information Technology degrees and diplomas, it might be confusing and challenging to choose the best suited educational provider for you.

Since we represent many universities that offer these courses, we will provide you with a list of candidate universities and you will only need to take the final decision in accordance with the information provided.

Available Courses


  • Diploma of Information Technology
  • Graduate Diploma in Information Technology
  • Bachelor of Information Technology
  • Advanced Diploma of Information Technology
  • Associate Degree in Information Technology
  • Master of Information Technology Accounting

The Australian Computer Society is the professional association responsible for the Information and Communication Technology sector in Australia. Having an accredited degree from a reputed university is the pathway to gain associate membership from the Australian Computer Society.


Engineering

The Engineering profession is among the most upbeat and evolving fields of study that covers a wide range of fields and skills. From website developers to mechanical and civil construction work, there are many different career paths for an engineer with a high amount of global opportunities.

Based on 2018-2019 Times Higher Education World University Rankings’ Engineering and Technology, Australia has 7 of the top 100 universities for studying engineering.

Additionally, Australian degrees and qualifications are internationally recognized, suitable for overseas job prospects.

With Australia’s need for more engineers, graduates of engineering courses are able to take advantage of the demand. Engineering degree/qualification holders of Australian education are given priority during recruitment.

Previously, Engineering had four main disciplines, chemical, electrical, civil, and mechanical. Engineering now consists of many disciplines as a result of the increase of aspirants with a wide range of innovative ideas.

Since we represent many universities that offer this wide range of courses, we will provide you with a list of candidate universities and you will only need to take the final decision in accordance with the information provided.

Available Courses


  • Diploma of Engineering
  • Bachelor of Engineering (with specialisations)
  • Master of Engineering (with specialisations)
  • Associate Degree in Engineering
  • Graduate Diploma in Engineering
  • Doctor of Philosophy - Engineering Accounting
  • Associate Degree in Engineering

The association responsible for the Engineering sector in Australia is the Institute of Engineers Australia. To secure the membership of Engineers Australia, one should hold an accredited degree from a recognized university or and institute and should have a minimum of 3 years of work experience in the engineering sector.
